Yidong Song

I am a PhD student at Sun Yat-sen University, focusing my research on AI4Science, particularly in the areas of protein structures and protein functions. I have published several high-quality papers in prestigious journals, including Nature Communications, Briefings in Bioinformatics, Nucleic Acids Research, and European Radiology. In 2023, I had the honor of having my work featured as the cover article in the Synthetic Biology Journal. Additionally, I serve as a reviewer for several academic journals, including Briefings in Bioinformatics, NAR Genomics and Bioinformatics, and Bioinformatics Advances.

In 2024, I was awarded the President’s Scholarship and a competitive National Scholarship from Sun Yat-sen University. I have achieved a National Second Prize in China Undergraduate Mathematical Contest in Modeling in 2019 and was selected for the Tencent 2024 Rhinoceros Bird Elite Talent Program, which recognizes only about 50individuals nationwide.
